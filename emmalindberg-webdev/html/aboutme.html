family the lindbergs

With a busy family, it isn’t too often we get pictures all together! This one is from my high school graduation back in 2019. I am the second oldest of six children born to amazing parents, Van and Susan Lindberg. My parents inspire me to be a better person and to reach my professional goals.

faith my mission

I recently returned home from my full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I had the opportunity to serve in the Brazil Natal Mission, located in the Northeastern region of Brazil, and in the Massachusetts Boston Mission, located in the New England area of the US. My faith is very important to me, and sharing it with others was an experience I will never forget.

fun around the world

One of my favorite things to do is travel! Whether it be through the countrysides and cathedrals of Europe, the beaches and jungles of Southeast Asia, or even the National Parks here in the US, I learn something new every time in every place.
